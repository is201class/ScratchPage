Recipe:

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 F
  2. Mix Together Dry Ingredients & Set Aside
    • 1 & 1/8 cup of Flour
    • 1/2 tsp of Salt
    • 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda
  3. Beat Sugar and Butter with ELectric Mixer
    • 1 c of Brown Sugar
    • 1/2 cup of White Sugar
    • 12 tbs. of utter
  4. Add the Rest of the Wet Ingredients
    • 2 tsp of Vanilla
    • 1 egg
    • 1 egg yolk
  5. Mix Wet & Dry Ingredients together
  6. Add Chocolate Chips
      7. 2 cups is reccomended, but measure that by your heart!
  7. Roll into 1 in Spheres & Place on Greases Pan
  8. Bake for 11-12 Mins
  9. Cool for 5 Mins & ENJOY!
